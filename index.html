About BT Management SARL

BT Management SARL is an independent sports management and advisory company focused on sports business operations, commercial development, marketing strategy, and investment structuring within the global sports ecosystem.

The company does not act as an athlete agent or representative. BT Management SARL operates exclusively on the business, commercial, and investment side of sport.

We support sports organisations, projects, and investors with structured, conflict-free advisory services, combining operational expertise, commercial intelligence, and institutional discipline.
